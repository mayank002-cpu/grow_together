Why We Built Grow Together
Our Mission

We are two Class 12 students who decided to treat board exam preparation like a team sport.

Instead of just saying “kal se padhenge”, we created a system that forces us to:

  • Fix a clear daily routine
  • Give each other mini-tests
  • Track our points and progress honestly
  • Review weekly targets and adjust

This website is not a coaching or institute. It’s just our promise to future us: we did everything we could.
