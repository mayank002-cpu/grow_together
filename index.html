Our Mission
Why this site exists

Two friends, one board exam season. We decided to treat preparation like a team sport instead of a solo fight.

  • Fix a clear daily routine and stick to it
  • Give each other mini-tests and check copies
  • Track scores and declare a weekly winner
  • Stay honest – no fake checkmarks, no fake study

This website is not coaching. It is just our promise to future us that we did everything we could.
